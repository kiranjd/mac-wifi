## 9. Asset interpretation guidance

When these assets are used in research, the system should pay attention to:

- whether the UI feels like a troubleshooting tool, a menu replacement, or a confidence utility
- whether the screenshots communicate value quickly enough to non-technical users
- whether the visuals over-index on "developer utility" instead of "problem solved"
- whether the social exports suggest a clearer audience than the website copy does
