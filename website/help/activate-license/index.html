Activate your MacWiFi license

If you bought MacWiFi already, you only need your license key and the app installed on your Mac.

1. Find your license key

Check your purchase email for the MacWiFi license key. If you used the one-click activation button in that email, the app should open and activate automatically.

2. Open the license screen inside MacWiFi

Open MacWiFi from the menu bar. If this Mac is not activated yet, the app now opens directly into the license screen and keeps diagnostics locked until activation succeeds. You can also open the dedicated license window from the menu bar app menu.

3. Paste the key and activate

Paste the key, press Activate, and MacWiFi will link the license to this Mac. After that, the app will validate the entitlement quietly in the background.

Need help?

If the key does not activate, email support@macwifi.live and include the exact error message from the app.
