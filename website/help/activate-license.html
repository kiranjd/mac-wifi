Activate Your MacWiFi License

Support guide

After purchase, MacWiFi sends an email with a download link and an activation link. You can activate in either of these ways:

  1. Click the activation link in the email. If MacWiFi is installed, it should open the app with your key.
  2. Or copy the license key from the email and paste it into MacWiFi settings.

Open settings in the app

Open the MacWiFi menu bar popover and click Buy / Activate… or Manage License… in the footer.

If the link does not open the app

  • Make sure MacWiFi is installed first.
  • Try clicking the activation link again from the purchase email.
  • If that still fails, paste the key manually into settings.

If activation fails

  • Double-check the key for missing characters.
  • Try a different network if your current one is restrictive.
  • Email support@macwifi.live if the issue continues.
